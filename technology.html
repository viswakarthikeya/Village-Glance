Spirulina Cultur

Spirulina is a type of blue-green algae that is widely used as a nutritional supplement due to its high protein, vitamin, and mineral content. It is also used in the production of cosmetics and animal feed. Spirulina cultivation can be done in both open and closed systems, depending on the resources available and the scale of production.

Biofloc Cultvation

Biofloc technology (BFT) is a sustainable aquaculture practice that uses microorganisms to convert organic waste into protein-rich feed for aquatic animals.

Honey Culture

Honey culture, also known as beekeeping, is the practice of raising and managing honey bees for the production of honey, beeswax, and other bee-related products
